## Overview

The **Litter-Robot 4** is the latest version of the internet's most famous self-cleaning litter box. It automatically sifts waste into a sealed drawer after each use, tracks your cat's bathroom habits via app, and promises you'll never scoop again.

At $699, it better be life-changing. After 6 weeks with two cats, here's our take.

## Key Features

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Cleaning | Automatic after each use, 7-minute delay |
| Capacity | Waste drawer holds 7-10 days (1 cat) |
| Connectivity | Wi-Fi, app with health insights |
| Cat weight | Supports cats 3-25 lbs |
| Odor control | Sealed drawer + carbon filter |
| Night light | Built-in, auto-sensing |

## What We Loved

**You genuinely never scoop.** After the novelty wore off, we realized how much mental load it removes. No daily scooping, no litter box guilt when you come home late. Empty the drawer every 7-10 days, that's it.

**Odor control is excellent.** The sealed drawer contains smell far better than any covered box we've used. Guests couldn't tell we had cats. The carbon filter does real work here.

**Health tracking** caught an issue. The app flagged that one of our cats was using the box significantly more frequently over a 3-day period. A vet visit confirmed a minor UTI. Without the data, we wouldn't have noticed for days.

**It's quieter than previous versions.** The LR3 sounded like a garbage disposal. The LR4 is a gentle hum that doesn't spook either of our cats.

## What Could Be Better

**$699 is a LOT.** There's no way around it. You can buy 5+ years of regular litter boxes for this price. You need to really hate scooping (or have multiple cats) to justify it.

**It's huge.** At 29 inches tall and 25 inches wide, it dominates any room it's in. If you live in a small apartment, measure twice before buying.

**Litter type matters.** It only works well with clumping clay litter. If you use crystal, pine, or paper litter, this isn't for you.

**The app has occasional disconnects.** About once a week, we'd get a "device offline" notification that resolved itself within an hour. Minor, but annoying for a $700 product.

## Who Should Buy This

- Multi-cat households (the time savings multiply)
- People with mobility issues who struggle with scooping
- Anyone who travels frequently and doesn't want to burden a pet sitter
- Cat owners who are serious about odor control

**Skip it if:** you're on a budget, live in a tiny space, or use non-clumping litter.

## Verdict

The Litter-Robot 4 delivers on its core promise: you will never scoop litter again. The health tracking is a genuine bonus that justifies the "smart" label. But $699 is premium pricing, and the size will rule it out for many. If you can afford it and have the space, it's an excellent quality-of-life upgrade.

**Rating: 4/5** — Life-changing convenience at a price that stings.
