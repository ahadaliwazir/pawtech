## What Is the Petcube Cam 2?

The Petcube Cam 2 is a compact wi-fi pet camera designed for pet owners who want to keep an eye on their furry friends without spending a fortune. At $49, it's one of the most affordable dedicated pet cameras on the market — and it delivers surprisingly solid performance for the price.

## Design & Setup

The Petcube Cam 2 is tiny. It's about the size of a tennis ball, wrapped in a matte black housing that blends into most shelves or entertainment centers. Setup takes under five minutes — download the Petcube app, connect to your wi-fi, and you're streaming live video.

The magnetic base is a nice touch. You can stick it to a fridge, mount it on a metal shelf, or just set it on a flat surface. There's no bulky stand or complicated mounting hardware to deal with.

## Video & Audio Quality

For a $49 camera, the video quality is impressive. The 1080p stream is sharp during the day, and the night vision mode switches on automatically in low light. You won't get the crystal clarity of a Furbo or Blink, but it's more than adequate for checking in on your pet.

Two-way audio works reliably. There's a slight delay (maybe half a second), but your pet will hear you clearly. The built-in speaker is louder than expected — our test cat definitely perked up when we called her name.

## Smart Features & Alerts

Here's where the subscription question comes in. The free tier gives you basic motion detection and live streaming. But if you want smart alerts that distinguish between your pet moving and a shadow shifting, you'll need Petcube Care at $5 per month.

The subscription also unlocks video history (up to 10 days), sound alerts, and activity zones. It's not mandatory, but the smart filtering makes a noticeable difference in reducing false notifications.

## Who Should Buy This?

The Petcube Cam 2 is perfect for pet owners who want basic monitoring without a big investment. If you just need to check in during the workday, hear what's going on, and get alerts when your pet is active, this camera delivers.

## The Verdict

At $49, the Petcube Cam 2 punches well above its weight. You lose the bells and whistles of premium cameras — no treat tossing, no 360° rotation, no auto-tracking — but you gain an affordable, reliable way to watch your pet from anywhere. For budget-conscious pet parents, it's an easy recommendation.
