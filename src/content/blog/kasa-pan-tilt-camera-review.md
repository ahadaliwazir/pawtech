## What is the Kasa Smart Camera?

The Kasa 1080p Indoor Pan/Tilt Wired Security Camera is a versatile smart home device that doubles exceptionally well as a pet monitor. With motion detection, 2-way audio, and night vision, it offers features usually found in much more expensive dedicated pet cameras.

## Our Honest Thoughts

This security camera is a game changer! Perfect for keeping an eye on the little ones or pets.

The pan and tilt functionality allows you to scan the whole room, so your pet can't hide in a blind spot. While it doesn't have a treat tosser like the Furbo, at this price point, it is an unbeatable value for basic monitoring.
